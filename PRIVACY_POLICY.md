# Privacy Policy – Church Finder (Youth Montreal)

**Last updated:** April 7, 2026

Youth Montreal ("we", "our", "us") built Church Finder to help people discover hosts and events in Montreal and surrounding regions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our website and mobile app.

## 1) Scope

This policy applies to:

- Church Finder web application
- Church Finder Android application
- Future iOS application

If a specific platform behaves differently, we will describe those differences in this document.

## 2) Information We Collect

### A. Information you provide directly

We may collect:

- Contact details you submit (for example, email when contacting us or applying to become a host)
- Report/request content you send through forms
- Any optional profile or account information (if account features are enabled)

### B. Approximate location data

With your permission, we access **approximate location** to:

- Set the default map center around your area
- Improve in-app suggestions (for example, nearby hosts/events)

Important notes:

- Location access is optional and permission-based.
- You can deny location permission and still use core app features, though suggestions and default map centering may be less accurate.
- We aim to use approximate location, not precise GPS coordinates, unless explicitly required and disclosed later.

### C. Personal calendar access (future functionality)

With your explicit permission, the app may access your personal agenda/calendar to:

- Create events in your personal calendar from events shown in Church Finder
- Modify calendar entries created by Church Finder
- Delete calendar entries created by Church Finder

Important notes:

- Calendar access is optional and disabled unless you grant permission.
- We do **not** need access to unrelated personal calendar events to provide this feature.
- If technical platform constraints require broader calendar permission scopes, we will minimize processing and only act on entries connected to Church Finder actions.

### D. Technical and usage data

We may collect limited technical data required for operations and security, such as:

- Device type, operating system, app version
- Basic logs for diagnostics, abuse prevention, and performance

## 3) How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and operate app features
- Show relevant hosts/events and map results
- Process user-submitted reports and host requests
- Support admin moderation and audit workflows
- Improve reliability, security, and product quality
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

## 4) Legal Bases (where applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, we rely on one or more of:

- Your consent (e.g., location/calendar permissions)
- Legitimate interests (security, maintenance, service improvement)
- Contractual necessity (delivering requested service)
- Legal obligations

You can withdraw consent at any time by revoking app permissions in your device settings.

## 5) Sharing of Information

We may share data with:

- Service providers that host or process data on our behalf (e.g., infrastructure, analytics, communication tools)
- Authorized admins and hosts, strictly as needed for operations (e.g., reviewing reports)
- Authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 6) Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for:

- The purposes described in this policy
- Legal, accounting, and security obligations
- Resolving disputes and enforcing agreements

Retention periods may vary by data type and legal requirements.

## 7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8) Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete data
- Restrict or object to certain processing
- Withdraw consent
- Request data portability

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

## 9) Children

Church Finder is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If we learn we collected personal data from a child without valid consent, we will take steps to delete it.

## 10) International Transfers

If data is processed outside your province/state/country, we will apply reasonable safeguards required by law.

## 11) Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes may be communicated through the app or website.
